Juita Teja

I first drew this figure with the intention of joining an open competition of all ages in 2022, where contestants must choose a historical figure from Malacca Sultanate era driven from Hikayat. I was inspired by a book named 'Nirnama' written by Hilal Asyraf, a beautiful-high-maintenance of a woman from Pahang, her name is Tun Teja Ratna Benggala. I even wrote a poem after her story.

REFERENCE

I found this outfit of Tun Teja in a drawing. Saturating the color here suits her personality.

DRAWING

A single sketch already took hours to draw and take proportions right, but really coloring this also gave me a reflective challenge :’)

EDITING

To add layers of lighting and color correction to ensure my piece holds a gentle harmony — I also chose to focus on the skin color.
