Pendapa

These three children are my original characters (OC) which I kept use in most of my post. From the left, Milo, Hamzah, and Amy. Creating a backstory and lore intertwined between characters, connecting them together for me to create an emotional attachment to my creations! This was my first ever full drawn colored piece in digital art, everything was drawn by a single finger back in 2021.

REFERENCE

There wasn’t really much reference used, except for the use of color palletes. This is where I learn about mood and tones in a picture.

DRAWING

Back then I don’t have any access to any digital tools except for my very own tablet and a passion to express myself through my characters.

EDITING

Added layers of lighting and overlays to create depth in my drawing especially for the background.
