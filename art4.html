Rumah Pokok

I have always loved the concept of traditional Malay’s house. I dream of creating yet another nostalgic background for my characters backstory. I drew this inside of my dorm back when I was in form 4, right away leaving my mathematics problem as soon as I was inspired to draw. It’s just a canon event for any artist to get creative in the most critical times. Reminded of the reference I saw the other day, I started sketching purely based off memories and imagination. Then, I just started praying it’ll work out as I trusted the process.

REFERENCE

I wasn’t searching for anything specific in particular when I found a concept art of a tree house. I thought why not improve it?

SKETCHING

Back then I don’t have any access to any digital tools except for my very own tablet and a passion to express myself through my characters.

INKING

Added layers of lighting and overlays to create depth in my drawing especially for the background.
